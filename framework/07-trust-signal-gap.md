# The Trust Signal Gap

Many organisations invest heavily in cybersecurity.

Despite this investment, their digital presence often emits weak or inconsistent signals of trust.

This disconnect is the **Trust Signal Gap**.

> Organisations spend millions on cybersecurity but still emit weak trust signals.

## Why the gap exists

Trust-relevant systems are often managed across multiple teams:

- marketing controls domains
- technology controls infrastructure
- procurement selects SaaS vendors
- comms owns channels and platforms

Without a unified view of the Trust Surface, trust signals degrade unnoticed.

## Closing the gap

Closing the gap requires organisations to:

- identify the Trust Surface
- measure Trust Signals
- strengthen weak signals
- integrate trust posture into governance

TSF provides a structured model for doing this.
