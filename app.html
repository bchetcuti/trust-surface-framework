The web app (optional implementation)

TrustSurface is a conceptual framework first. The web app exists to make the documents easy to browse, link, and keep consistent.

What the app does

  • Searchable, linkable library of framework documents.
  • Stable URLs for reference in board papers and governance artefacts.
  • SVG diagram asset suitable for slides and PDF export.

What the app does not do (yet)

  • No scoring automation by default.
  • No feature creep; the framework stabilises first.
