Trust Surface Framework (TSF)

Understanding and governing the digital signals organisations emit. TSF is a governance model to identify the digital systems where trust is experienced, measure the signals they broadcast, and improve trust posture over time.

For boards: start with Board Questions. For practitioners: start with Framework Map.

Most organisations invest heavily in cybersecurity — yet still emit weak trust signals at the digital edge: domains, email, services, platforms, and vendors.

Organisations spend millions on cybersecurity but still emit weak trust signals.

Trust Surface: where trust is experienced
Trust Signals: observable indicators of trust posture
Lifecycle: Discover → Assess → Harden → Govern → Signal
